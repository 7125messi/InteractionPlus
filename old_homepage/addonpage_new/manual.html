Manual of Interaction+

1. Basic Exploring Loop

Figure shows the basic complete loop of exploration from filtering in a defined region to the exploring share. It contains 6 steps:
  • Step 1: Click the Interaction+ on bookbar: to begin the filtering with a click.
  • Step 2: Drag a region: to define the region of interest by click-and-drag.
  • Step 3: Click filtering objects: to select the filtering object.
  • Step 4: Brush on the properties: to define the filtering constraints on the properties.
  • Step 5: Share exploration: to submit the exploration publically.
  • Step 6: Check others' exploration: to explore others' submission further.

Figure 1: Basic Exploring Loop

2. Filtering Functions

The filtering panel contains three types of functions:
  • Region Functions: to remove, shrink or share the exploration.
  • Object Functions: to manipulate the objects, such remove the unnecessary the object groups, retrieve them back, compose them logic operations etc.
  • Property Functions: to manipulate and set the filtering constraints, such as deriving properties based on exist ones etc.
